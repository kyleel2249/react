Earlier this week GHC15 trended. What started as a mere suggestion on saving tips to help guys give their girlfriends befitting birthday presents turned out to have infuriated the guys instead. Reading some of the comments, I wondered if some of these guys had girlfriends. It\u2019ll be disheartening to see your boyfriend\u2019s tweet suggesting that women don\u2019t deserve all that and more. Agreed, Ghc15 a day summing up to Ghc5,475 a year may seem a bit much to spend in one day especially if you don\u2019t have that much to spare but maybe, just maybe this whole thing has been taken out of context. I think all she was trying to say is, put a bit more effort into gifting. Knowing birthdays are an annual thing, it\u2019ll be wise to save towards them just like we save towards every other thing. It\u2019s an investment into your relationship.\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

Even when we say we want a lowkey birthday this year,\u00a0 we still want you to put in effort. Remember when they said women never say what they mean? Yeah, this is one instance.<\/p>\n
