They (Vodafone) put a lot of effort into creating a better future at Vodafone, a world that is more interconnected, diverse, and sustainable. Their ability to accomplish this as a dynamic global society is a result of the human spirit combined with technology.<\/p>\n

In order to link individuals, organizations, and communities around the world, they innovate and pose challenges, as well as experiment, pick things up quickly, and work together to complete tasks in order to please consumers and win their loyalty.<\/p>\n

With Vodafone, you can be who you really are, feel like you belong, inspire others, seize opportunities, prosper, and actually change the world.<\/p>\n

About The Vodafone Ghana Discover Graduate Programme<\/b><\/h2>\n

Remarkable things to enable everyone to feel confidently connected are done in Vodafone. These initiatives range from giving smartphones superfast network speeds to facilitating mobile money payments for farmers or streaming music into a car via the Internet of Things. Vodafone has a really vibrant and varied mix of skills and people as a result, which makes us a terrific place to work.<\/span><\/p>\n

Vodafone is searching for individuals who have a creative mindset, embrace new technology,
\nare lifelong learners, and are eager to explore the opportunities offered to them. As you launch your career, Vodafone is a place where you can be wholly present at work.\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

What’s in it for you?<\/span><\/b><\/h2>\n
    \n
  • Investment in your personal development<\/span><\/li>\n
  • Opportunity to work for an industry-leading company that spans across different countries around the world<\/span><\/li>\n
  • Having business exposure and accountability from day one<\/span><\/li>\n
  • Being part of a global community of graduates<\/span><\/li>\n
  • Work to deliver leading technology to empower both Enterprise and Consumer customers around the world to be confidently and securely connected<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    What to expect<\/span><\/b><\/h2>\n
      \n
    • Up to 3 cross-functional rotations<\/span><\/li>\n
    • Training & personal development<\/span><\/li>\n
    • Support to fulfill your potential and career goals<\/span><\/li>\n
    • Build expertise in your chosen career path<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

      What Vodafone Is Looking For<\/span><\/b><\/h2>\n
        \n
      • Demonstrate creativity and adaptability<\/span><\/li>\n
      • Passion for customers and commercial savviness<\/span><\/li>\n
      • Very good academic record evidenced by<\/span>\n

        • Master\u2019s Degree earned or on track to receive one by September 2023<\/span><\/li>\n
        • National Service Completion by October\/November 2023<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n
        • Critical thinking, initiative, and analytical skills<\/span><\/li>\n
        • Proficient in English<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

          \u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n

          Scope of Program<\/span><\/b><\/h1>\n

          Digital Route<\/span><\/u><\/b><\/h2>\n

          The world is changing fast and Vodafone is one of the top telecom brands across the globe that is contributing towards connecting people, businesses, and communities in the digital world.\u00a0 With the internet, digital technology, and mobile innovation driving unimaginable change, there\u2019s no better time to
          \nbuild a career in Agile & Digital.<\/span><\/p>\n

          Digital & Agile fields you can choose from include: Mobile & Web Development, Digital Architecture, Data Science, and Business Analysis, among others<\/span><\/p>\n

          Commercial Route<\/span><\/u><\/b><\/h2>\n

          In Vodafone, the\u00a0Commercial<\/b> unit seeks to satisfy the needs of individual consumers, enterprises, and small businesses. This is done through the provision of voice and data packages, fixed lines, and broadband and mobile money services.<\/span><\/p>\n

          Whatever you excel at \u2013 whether it’s analysis of facts and figures, building business cases, customer segmentation, product development, selling solutions, or supporting business-critical decisions we have a role for you.<\/span><\/p>\n

          Areas you could find yourself working in Sales, Product Development, Segments, Brands, and Business Analysis.<\/span><\/p>\n

          With the adventure of self-discovery that the two-year Discover programme offers why not come on an incredible journey with Vodafone? Ready?<\/span><\/i><\/p>\n

          \u00a0<\/span><\/i><\/p>\n

          Application process<\/span><\/b><\/h2>\n

          When you apply for the Vodafone Graduate Programme you will be asked to fill out an application form. This allows us to get to know you and your background of experience. Here you will be asked to attach your\u00a0CV<\/b> in English.<\/span><\/p>\n

          If your background and experience match the requirements, you will be invited to participate in our fully virtual talent acquisition process. This allows us to see if you are the right fit for the Vodafone culture. The online assessment includes the following:<\/span><\/p>\n

            \n
          • Potential Indicator, Motivation, and Competencies Interview<\/span><\/b>.\u00a0Please complete your video interview in English.<\/b><\/span><\/li>\n
          • Gamification-based cognitive assessment\u00a0<\/span><\/b>will be applied to the assessment<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
              \n
            • After the online assessment, the most successful candidates will be invited to an\u00a0Assessment
              \nCentre (<\/b>This includes individual and group case study presentations to a panel)<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

              Application Deadline: 24th May 2023<\/i><\/a><\/span><\/h2>\n

              Vodafone is dedicated to attracting, nurturing, and keeping the best employees by providing an inspiring and welcoming workplace where talent is properly acknowledged and rewarded. With the conviction that
              \ndiversity is crucial to the success of our company, we are dedicated to encouraging inclusion for all. Everyone is strongly encouraged to think about joining the adventure.<\/p>\n
