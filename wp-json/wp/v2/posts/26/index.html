WhatsApp just released a new update for iOS and it’s got some exciting new features! Learn about the latest WhatsApp update for iOS, featuring the widely-anticipated sticker maker tool.<\/p>\n

\ud83d\udcf1 The build number is 23.7.82 – have you updated yet?<\/p>\n

One of the most exciting additions to this update is the new sticker maker tool. This allows users to create custom stickers right within the app, without having to switch to a different third-party app. It’s a great way to save time and make the process of creating stickers more integrated and seamless.<\/p>\n

Have you tried out the new sticker maker tool yet? What kind of stickers would you create?<\/p>\n

The process is simple: just extract a subject from an image and paste it into a chat. WhatsApp will then automatically convert the image into a sticker that can be added to your collection. It’s a fun and creative way to personalize your chats and express yourself.<\/p>\n

Note that the sticker maker tool is rolling out to all users on iOS 16 and newer, but it won’t be available on older versions of iOS. So, make sure you’ve updated your app to the latest version to enjoy this feature!<\/p>\n
