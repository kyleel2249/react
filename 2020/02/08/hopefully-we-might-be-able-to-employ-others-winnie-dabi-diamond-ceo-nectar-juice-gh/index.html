Hopefully we might be able to employ others – Winnie Dabi Diamond (CEO – Nectar Juice Gh)

Joseph Anim Darko3 years ago4 mins ago26 mins
 
 
Winnie Dabi Diamond and her partner in business, Judith Asampana started a business right after their National Service with the idea whiles studying on campus to build up a business for themselves and then as part of contributing to society, employ others to control the unemployment rate and further boost the business.

The brain behind Nectar Juice Gh

In an interview with the co-founder of Nectar Juice Gh, Ms Dabi Diamond said the business will go a long way to survive and employ others to help control the unemployment rate in the country just as other business owners might help to do the same.
Winnie Dabi Diamond is a young lady who graduated from the University of professional studies, Accra with a BSc. Business Administration (Degree), the co-founder of Nectar Juice Gh.
Her partner in the Nectar juice business is Judith Asampana, a graduate of Lancaster University with a Law degree.
Winnie & Judith

She is Ghanaian from the Volta region but has lived in Accra all her life. She is a very determined person, very goal oriented and really loves to push people to do things they have always wanted to do.

The Journey

She noticed over the years that she’s a very handy person; I mean, I’m really good at doing things with her hands, a visual learner and learnt a lot through that as well as practical means. That is how she realized that she’s a “jack of all trades.”

Over the years she started learning a lot of handy works that could easily bring money to the table. First was makeup, wig making and then making of juices. Along the way, what came up next was a little buying and selling.  Currently, her main focus is on the juice business although she does the others part-time.

Taking Off

The juice business started as a result of her latter days during service as the thought of not being retained at her place of service.

As a result, she started thinking of other means to sustain or support herself right after National Service so she approached a very good friend of hers and pitched in the idea of both of us coming together to bring this dream into reality.

Basically, at Nectar Juice, they produce fresh fruit juices for all occasions; weddings, naming ceremonies, parties, corporate meetings or events and many more. They also sell to individuals and sell at fairs as well.
They look out for the best for our customers in regard to their health. Also, they make sure all their juices a naturally made with no added preservatives or any chemical, giving customers a wide range of flavours and tastes which is 100% natural.
Natural flavours

 

Some of the fruits used for the juices are pineapple, watermelon, mango, guava with a mixture of some vegetables such as beetroot, carrot, dandelion, etc.
Recently, they have started with the service of providing locally Ghanaian drinks such as Bissap juice (sobolo), Asana, Lamugin and palm wine to their menu.
Satisfying customer demands
For an “exotic feel,” they also provide fruit punches, alcoholic punches and alcoholic cocktails to their menu.

The Plan

When asked about their plans for Nectar Juice Gh, she explained, “Currently, we work from home at Ashaley Botwe-New town and at the moment we are focused on raising enough capital to be able to cater for certain things and hopefully we might be able to employ others.”
